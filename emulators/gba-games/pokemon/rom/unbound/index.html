Pokemon Unbound

---------------

Pokémon Unbound is filled with many features like difficulty settings, A new mission system, Max raids, and more. It is a GBA Rom Hack of Fire Red with Pokémon from Generations I to VIII, and Gen VIII game mechanics. It is set in fanfictional region called the Borrius Region and has a complex plot. Hoopa Unbound is important to the plot and the mascot Pokemon
